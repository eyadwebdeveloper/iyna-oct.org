IYNA Oct. Summer program

By applying to this form, you are applying to join our academics committee. In the academics committee, you will share in the process of spreading neuroscience among high school students. You will take part in material preparation, neuroscience explanation, preparing for our neuroscience summer competition, as well as other activities made by the chapter. The application process compromises two stages, this application and an interview. Shortlisted applicants will be emailed with the interview information. The applicant with the best application will be assigned the head of the academics committee.

Application deadline is on 15th of August at 11:59 PM
